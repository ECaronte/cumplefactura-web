# Project Summary
CumpleFactura is a SaaS platform designed for digital freelancers and WooCommerce stores, offering automated legal invoicing, PDF generation, and compliance with Spanish regulations. It optimizes operations for freelancers, micro-enterprises, online academies, WordPress agencies, and management firms. The platform integrates with WooCommerce through the VerifactuWoo plugin and provides extensive resources on electronic invoicing and compliance via its blog.

# Project Module Description
The project includes the following functional modules:
- **Home Page**: Overview of CumpleFactura and its features.
- **How It Works**: Details on WooCommerce integration and automated invoicing.
- **Plugin Landing**: Insights on the VerifactuWoo plugin and its installation.
- **Pricing**: Information on pricing plans and features.
- **Agency**: Information tailored for agencies and their benefits.
- **Resources**: A collection of legal and technical resources for users.
- **Blog**: Articles on electronic invoicing and legal compliance.
- **Legal Index**: A page linking to all legal documents.
- **Legal Notice**: Information about site ownership, usage, and terms.
- **Privacy Policy**: Details on personal data handling per RGPD and LOPDGDD.
- **Cookies Policy**: Information on cookies used by CumpleFactura.
- **Terms and Conditions**: Usage conditions for CumpleFactura services and website.
