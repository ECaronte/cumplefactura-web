# Project Summary
CumpleFactura is a SaaS solution tailored for digital freelancers and WooCommerce stores, offering automated legal invoicing, PDF generation, and compliance with Spanish regulations. The platform integrates with WooCommerce via the VerifactuWoo plugin, enabling users to generate invoices and maintain records effortlessly. It serves a variety of clients, including freelancers, micro-enterprises, online academies, WordPress agencies, and management firms, significantly improving operational efficiency through streamlined invoicing and compliance processes.

# Project Module Description
The project consists of several functional modules:
- **Home Page**: Overview of CumpleFactura, its benefits, and a call to action.
- **How It Works**: Integration process with WooCommerce and automated workflow.
- **Plugin Landing**: Information about the VerifactuWoo plugin, its features, and installation instructions.
- **Pricing**: Transparent pricing plans with features and calls to action.
- **Agency**: Information tailored for agencies and their benefits.
- **Resources**: Guides, FAQs, and other helpful documentation.
