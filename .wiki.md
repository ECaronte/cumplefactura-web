# Project Summary
CumpleFactura is a SaaS platform designed for freelancers and WooCommerce businesses, focusing on automated legal invoicing, PDF generation, and compliance with Spanish regulations. It enhances operational efficiency for freelancers, micro-enterprises, online academies, and WordPress agencies by streamlining electronic invoicing and providing educational resources via its blog. Recent updates have improved user experience, ensured compliance with "Factura Electrónica 2026," integrated "Firma XAdES" for digital signatures, and implemented user-consent-based Google Analytics. The platform's homepage has been optimized to better serve freelancers and online businesses.

# Project Module Description
The project includes the following functional modules:
- **Home Page**: Overview of CumpleFactura, focusing on freelancers and WooCommerce integration.
- **How It Works**: Details on WooCommerce integration and automated invoicing, now featuring a flow diagram.
- **Plugin Landing**: Features and benefits of the VerifactuWoo plugin.
- **Pricing**: Pricing plans and features, now includes a section for agencies.
- **Agency**: Integration details for agencies with ERPs.
- **Resources**: Articles & Guides, Verifactu, Factura Electrónica 2026, Firma XAdES, Integraciones, and Seguridad y Cumplimiento.
- **Blog**: Articles on electronic invoicing and compliance.
- **Legal Index**: Links to legal documents.
- **Legal Notice**: Site ownership and usage terms.
- **Privacy Policy**: Data handling details per RGPD and LOPDGDD.
- **Cookies Policy**: Information on cookies used by CumpleFactura.
- **Terms and Conditions**: Conditions for using CumpleFactura services.
- **Verifactu**: Explanation of the Verifactu standard.
- **Factura Electrónica 2026**: Requirements for electronic invoicing starting in 2026.
- **Firma XAdES**: Overview of the XAdES digital signature.
- **Integraciones**: Information on how CumpleFactura integrates with ERPs, now enhanced with detailed explanations on CSV/XML exports, visual examples, API options, and ERP compatibility.
- **Seguridad y Cumplimiento**: Security measures and compliance protocols.
