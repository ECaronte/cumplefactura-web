# Project Summary
CumpleFactura is a SaaS platform designed for digital freelancers and WooCommerce stores, offering automated legal invoicing, PDF generation, and adherence to Spanish regulations. It enhances operational efficiency for freelancers, micro-enterprises, online academies, WordPress agencies, and management firms. The platform integrates with WooCommerce through the VerifactuWoo plugin and provides educational resources on electronic invoicing and compliance via its blog. Recent enhancements include preparations for "Factura Electrónica 2026," the integration of "Firma XAdES" for digital signatures, user-consent-based Google Analytics integration, and new pages detailing ERP connections and security measures. The homepage has been updated to emphasize ERP compatibility and security features.

# Project Module Description
The project includes the following functional modules:
- **Home Page**: Overview of CumpleFactura and its features, now highlighting ERP compatibility and security.
- **How It Works**: Information on WooCommerce integration and automated invoicing.
- **Plugin Landing**: Insights on the VerifactuWoo plugin and its installation.
- **Pricing**: Details on pricing plans and features.
- **Agency**: Information tailored for agencies, emphasizing integration with ERPs via CSV/XML and API.
- **Resources**: Dropdown with links to Articles & Guides, Verifactu, Factura Electrónica 2026, Firma XAdES, Integraciones, and Seguridad y Cumplimiento.
- **Blog**: Articles on electronic invoicing and legal compliance.
- **Legal Index**: Links to all legal documents.
- **Legal Notice**: Information about site ownership, usage, and terms.
- **Privacy Policy**: Personal data handling details per RGPD and LOPDGDD.
- **Cookies Policy**: Information on cookies used by CumpleFactura.
- **Terms and Conditions**: Usage conditions for CumpleFactura services and website.
- **Verifactu**: Guide explaining the Verifactu standard and how CumpleFactura facilitates compliance.
- **Factura Electrónica 2026**: Requirements and implications of mandatory electronic invoicing starting in 2026.
- **Firma XAdES**: Explanation of the digital signature XAdES and its necessity in electronic invoicing.
- **Integraciones**: Detailed explanation of how CumpleFactura integrates with ERPs using CSV/XML and API.
- **Seguridad y Cumplimiento**: New page detailing security measures, data protection protocols, and compliance with legal obligations.
