# Project Summary
CumpleFactura is a SaaS platform tailored for freelancers and WooCommerce businesses, providing automated legal invoicing, PDF generation, and compliance with Spanish regulations. The platform significantly improves operational efficiency for freelancers, micro-enterprises, online academies, and WordPress agencies, while also offering educational resources on electronic invoicing through its blog. Recent updates focus on enhancing user experience for freelancers, with features like "Factura Electrónica 2026" compliance, "Firma XAdES" for digital signatures, and user-consent-based Google Analytics integration. The homepage has been refined to better highlight the primary audience of freelancers and online businesses.

# Project Module Description
The project includes the following functional modules:
- **Home Page**: Overview of CumpleFactura with a focus on freelancers and WooCommerce integration.
- **How It Works**: Details on WooCommerce integration and automated invoicing.
- **Plugin Landing**: Information on the VerifactuWoo plugin.
- **Pricing**: Pricing plans and features.
- **Agency**: Integration details for agencies with ERPs.
- **Resources**: Links to Articles & Guides, Verifactu, Factura Electrónica 2026, Firma XAdES, Integraciones, and Seguridad y Cumplimiento.
- **Blog**: Articles on electronic invoicing and compliance.
- **Legal Index**: Links to legal documents.
- **Legal Notice**: Site ownership and usage terms.
- **Privacy Policy**: Data handling details per RGPD and LOPDGDD.
- **Cookies Policy**: Information on cookies used by CumpleFactura.
- **Terms and Conditions**: Conditions for using CumpleFactura services.
- **Verifactu**: Explanation of the Verifactu standard.
- **Factura Electrónica 2026**: Requirements for electronic invoicing starting in 2026.
- **Firma XAdES**: Overview of the XAdES digital signature.
- **Integraciones**: How CumpleFactura integrates with ERPs.
- **Seguridad y Cumplimiento**: Security measures and compliance protocols.
